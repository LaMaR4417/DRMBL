1. Game Format

Clock & Quarters

  • Quarters 4 x 10 minutes
  • Clock (Q1–Q3) Running clock
  • Clock (Q4) Running clock; stoppages in last 3 minutes
  • Halftime 3 minutes
  • Between Quarters 1 minute

Timeouts

  • Per Team 2 full timeouts + 2 half (30-second) timeouts
  • Clock Timeouts stop the clock

Fouls & Free Throws

  • Personal Fouls 5 fouls per player
  • Team Bonus After 5th team foul per quarter (resets each quarter)
  • Lane Violations Enforced per NFHS

Overtime

  • Length 5 minutes
  • Clock Running clock; stoppages in last 1 minute
  • Timeouts 1 full timeout per team

General Rules

  • Shot Clock None
  • Dunking Not allowed (in-game or warm-ups)
  • Substitution Free subs on dead balls; players must announce in/out
  • Possession Jump ball at tip-off, then alternating possession
  • Backcourt 10-second rule enforced
  • Mercy Rule None
  • Warm-Up 5 minutes before each game
  • Game Ball League-provided

2. Season Structure & Schedule

Regular Season

  • Season Length 11 weeks (10 regular season + 1 playoff week)
  • Start Date April 2026
  • Registration Deadline March 20, 2026
  • Teams 12
  • Games Per Team 10 (guaranteed)
  • Game Days Sundays only
  • Game Times 8 AM – 2 PM (6 games per day, 1-hour slots)
  • Team Drops Remaining games become forfeits; opponents get free wins; past results still count

Playoffs

  • Format Single elimination
  • Qualification Top 4 teams
  • Seeding Win/loss record
  • Tiebreakers 1) Win/loss record → 2) Point differential (+/−) → 3) Head-to-head
  • Matchups #1 vs #4, #2 vs #3
  • Playoff Day Semi 1 → Semi 2 → 3rd Place → Championship
  • Championship Full stopped clock

3. Roster & Player Eligibility

Roster Rules

  • Roster Size 5–12 players per team
  • Roster Lock After week 5, rosters cannot be modified unless approved by the commissioner
  • Minimum to Play 4 players (game counts as a normal loss, not a forfeit)
  • One Team Only Players may only be on one team at a time

Eligibility

  • Ages 18+ (16–17 with signed waiver)
  • League Men’s only
  • Residency Open (no residency requirement)
  • Player ID QR code issued to every player

Free Agent Portal

  • Sign-Up Free — individuals can register to be picked up by teams
  • Pickup Fee $5 transfer fee when a team picks up a free agent

4. Conduct & Discipline

Technical Fouls & Ejections

  • Technicals 2 technical fouls = ejection
  • 1st Ejection 1-game suspension
  • 2nd Ejection 2-game suspension
  • 3rd Ejection Full season ban
  • Ejected Player Must leave facility at refs’ discretion

Fighting & Flagrants

  • Fighting 1st offense = suspended rest of season; 2nd offense = lifetime ban
  • Flagrant Fouls Per NFHS (refs’ discretion)

General Conduct

  • Spectators Disruptive fans will be removed; no team penalty
  • Substances No alcohol or substances allowed — zero tolerance
  • Appeals Players may appeal, but the commissioner has final say

5. Uniforms & Equipment

  • Jerseys Matching jerseys or matching colored shirts with numbers
  • Numbers Required — no number, no play
  • Jersey Deadline Must have them by week 3
  • Colors Teams should wear different colors when possible (no strict home/away rule)
  • Shoes Basketball or tennis shoes only
  • Jewelry No jewelry on the wrists (watches, bracelets); other jewelry allowed
  • Dress Code Shorts and numbered jersey/shirt — no jeans or pants

6. Forfeits & Scheduling

Forfeits

  • Grace Period 5 minutes before scheduled tipoff
  • No Notice 20–0 loss + 50-point deduction from point differential
  • With Notice 20–0 loss, no point deduction
  • Penalty No monetary fine — just the loss and point penalty

Scheduling

  • Rescheduling Possible with advance notice, but limited within the 11-week window
  • Facility Closure League will attempt to reschedule on a weekday

7. Registration & Fees

  • Team Fee $275
  • Late Registration $300 ($275 + $25 late fee)
  • Referee Fee $25 per team per game (each team pays $25 to one ref — 2 refs per game)
  • Ref Fee Due During the game; team won’t be scheduled next week if unpaid
  • Payment Methods Venmo, CashApp, Cash, Zelle
  • Refunds Only if not enough teams sign up to run the league

8. Transfers & Pickups

  • Transfers & Pickups Available until week 5 (roster is locked on week 6)
  • Transfer Fee $5 per player movement (adding or switching teams)
  • Who Pays Player or team — league just needs the $5
  • Limit No limit on additions — capped only by roster max (12)

9. Referees & Protests

  • Referees 2 per game
  • Decisions All referee decisions are final — no exceptions
  • Protests Protests will be allowed for rule violations, but not for referee calls
  • Stats Tracked Points, rebounds, assists, steals, blocks

10. Awards & Prizes

  • Awards TBD

  • Defensive Player Player vote — must submit the week’s vote by Wednesday of the following week
  • Scoring Champion Based on points per game (must participate in at least 7 games to be eligible)
